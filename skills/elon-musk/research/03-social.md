## 来源 1：@elonmusk 账号总览
- 类型：一手
- URL：https://x.com/elonmusk
- 关键发现：马斯克是Twitter/X最活跃的公众人物之一，粉丝超1.5亿。推文风格：短平快、meme化、直球、自嘲、不避争议。常在凌晨发布重大消息。

## 来源 3：特斯拉/DOGE相关推文

> "Am considering taking Tesla private at $420. Funding secured." (经典争议推文)
> "Dogecoin is the people's crypto"

## 来源 4：政治与言论自由立场

> "The woke mind virus is either defeated or nothing else matters."
> "I voted 100% Dem until a few years ago. Now, I think we need a red wave."

## 来源 5：AI/科技/未来讨论
